## 8. TONE & STYLE GUIDELINES

### Writing Principles:
✅ **DO:**
- Use clear, confident language
- Focus on client value and outcomes
- Be specific about what you deliver
- Emphasize reliability and professionalism
- Use concrete examples and metrics

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use generic phrases like "passionate developer"
- Focus only on technical skills without context
- Use jargon without explanation
- Make vague claims
- Sound desperate or salesy

### Voice Characteristics:
- **Professional** - Serious about craft and results
- **Confident** - Know your worth and expertise
- **Minimalist** - No fluff, straight to the point
- **Client-focused** - Always about their needs, not your ego
- **Approachable** - Technical but understandable
